Impact of Your Donations

Your contributions have made a significant difference in the lives of many animals. Here are some of the impacts:

  • Supported medical care for injured and sick animals.
  • Facilitated adoption programs that found homes for over 300 pets.
  • Raised awareness about animal welfare in the community.
